Sumin Eco Pavers

Sumin Eco Pavers: Shayna Eco Unified India Pvt. Ltd. Delhi based manufacturing company has dedicated its existence into transforming the adverse effects of plastics into composite and affordable structural tiles for societal benefit. Having a recyclable infrastructure in place and driven with a single point focus to turn plastics into high performance materials, Shayna Eco Unified India Pvt. Ltd. strives to deploy cost-effective architectural tiles further redirecting the recycling dimension of India towards an evolution. This has been possible through a golden handshake with the Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CSIR)-National Physical Laboratory’s (NPL), with whom this technology was developed, tested and certified for usage in multiple applications. These materials are weather resistant, chip resistant, acid proof, durable and offer better structural stability at a lower cost as compared to conventional materials in addition to protecting the environment from plastic dumping.

    Our products for trading under Sumin Eco Products are:
  • Recycled Plastic Pavers & Tiles
    • HDCP Paver tiles Light weight
    • HDCP Paver tiles heavy weight
    • Floor tiles with locks
    • Plain Structural tiles
    • Designer Wall tiles
    • Designer Structural tiles

  • Available Colours:
    • These all products are available in Eco-Friendly colours of Dark Blue & Dark Green.
    • But on demand of customers we also supply attractive colours like Light Blue, Light Green, Grey, Black, Coffee Brown, Terracotta and Orange.
  • Some int facts:
    • 600 waste plastic bags are recycled to make Single unit of 1Sq Feet (12”x12”) Floor Tile.
    • 400 waste plastic bags are recycled to make Single unit of Paver block.
  • Quality Control:

    • These HDCP Pavers & Tiles have several quality control tests that are used to meet product specifications and standardizations. The Pavers & Tiles have successfully passed through inspection test established by the ASTM (American Society of Testing and Materials) include tensile strength, flammability, thermal stability, compression strength, water absorption analysis, uv accelerated weathering, fire retardant, antistatic, zero halogen with no emission, glass transition temperature, permeability, environmental stability, resistance against strong acids & bases tests.


  • Load Bearing Capacity:

    • We have two varieties of HDCP pavers, the one weighing 250 gms (Heavy weight) has a load bearing capacity of 20 tonnes and the one weighing 200 gms (Light weight) has a load bearing capacity of 10 tonnes.


  • Laying/Installation of Pavers & Tiles:

    • For fixing tiles & paver using sand and cement follow the instructions given below:

    • Before starting the project, start with a dry run by laying out the tiles in the desired design/pattern (if using multiple colour tiles) on a plain levelled surface.
    • Use Cement and sand mortar in the ratio of 1:3. Add some water to create consistent paste
    • Once the mixing has been completed, DO NOT add more water and use mixture within one hour.
    • Apply a 15-20 mm layer of bedding material evenly spread over an area of 2’ x 2’ section/surface of floor/area at a time or as per directions of the site supervisor & engineer-in charge. Ensure that the tiled surface is levelled. Press gently for even adherence at the back of &. Do not use iron hammer. Always use wooden hammer to press the &
    • Lay out tiles from the centre of area in the desired design/pattern (if using multiple colour tiles) so that cut tiles, if any will be on the outer edges towards walls or side
    • Wait for 24-48 hours after fixing tiles. The area should be completely protected from pedestrian traffic or any subsequent disturbances for this period

    Please note the company does not accept any liability in case of problem arising out of non-adherence to these tile fixing instructions.
